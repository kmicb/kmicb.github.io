About Me

Who am I?

I am a classically trained pianist of over 40 years turned unapologetic raw mental health advocate. I was diagnosed with ADHD and a touch of the 'tism so I navigate life with some brilliance, clumsiness, and for sure no filter. Losing the majority of my hearing didn’t end music for me — felt like it did, but it didn't - it actually amplified it.

I orchestrate bold, unscripted conversations about mental health, social justice, identity, and neurodivergence. I fiercely oppose racism, homophobia, transphobia, capitalism, and all things unjust. If you’re here for polished fluff, you’re in the wrong concert hall.

I love dogs, cats (who knew?), science, physics, and the immortal wisdom of Carl Sagan. I live life with heart, humor, and a fire for equality that burns through every episode.
